Core Safety Features

Comprehensive protection through intelligent monitoring systems

Electric Field Detection

Custom probes detect live conductors up to 5 meters away, preventing electrical accidents before they happen.

Thermal Monitoring

IR sensors continuously scan for hot surfaces and overheating equipment, alerting workers to fire hazards.

Vital Signs Tracking

Heart rate and SpO₂ monitoring in the wrist cuff ensures worker health is constantly assessed.

Fall Detection

Advanced motion sensors detect falls and automatically alert supervisors with precise location data.

Multi-Modal Alerts

Vibration, sound, and visual alerts ensure warnings are noticed in any industrial environment.

IoT Connectivity

Seamless integration with existing safety systems through Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connectivity.
